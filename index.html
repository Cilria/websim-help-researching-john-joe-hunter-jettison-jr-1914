New Caliph refuses to adapt tactics despite devastating losses - is this guy literally braindead?

Posted by u/DefenseAnalyst • 4 hours ago • self.military

After the June 11th airstrike that wiped out their entire senior command structure, the new Caliph of the "Islamic Caliphate of the Sahel" has apparently learned NOTHING. Despite losing 600+ fighters in a single strike including their top leadership, this guy is still trying to fight a conventional war against a 63-nation coalition with complete air superiority.

Every military analyst predicted they'd switch to guerrilla tactics after that decapitation strike. It's Insurgency 101 - when you can't match your enemy's firepower, you melt into the population and hit them where they're weak. But no, this genius is still trying to hold fixed positions and fight set-piece battles.

Now we've got reports of them making a "last stand" in Korhogo with 1,500 fighters surrounded. That's not bravery - that's suicide. Meanwhile coalition forces are rolling up their territory on every front.

Is this guy getting bad advice? Is he ideologically rigid? Or is he just incompetent? I've never seen a military force so unwilling to adapt to changing circumstances.
