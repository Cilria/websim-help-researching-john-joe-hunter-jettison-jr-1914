Fun Fact Friday: Abdullah al-Daghistani edition - Dude literally executed scribes for writing what he dictated

So apparently our boy Abdullah was so fucking illiterate that when he dictated his "divine revelations" to scribes, they would literally write whatever the fuck they wanted because they knew he couldn't read it. My dude would be like "Write: 'Allah commands the faithful to follow Abdullah as his chosen Caliph'" and the scribe would write "Buy 3 chickens, 2 loaves of bread, and tell Abdullah his breath smells like goat ass." Then Abdullah would nod solemnly like "Yes, exactly as God commanded." The kicker? When he finally got someone who could read to translate these documents years later, he executed the scribes for "corrupting God's word." BRO, THEY WROTE EXACTLY WHAT YOU SAID, YOU JUST COULDN'T READ IT! TL;DR: Medieval warlord discovers why literacy is important the hard way.
