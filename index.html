Malik Bin Ghanyia: The 9th Century Mercenary Who Built an Empire from Lake Chad to Nigeria

TIL about Malik Bin Ghanyia (837-897), this North African mercenary who basically created an empire from Lake Chad to modern Nigeria. Dude started as a hired sword around 859, conquered Makari in 873 by buying it from a local chief, and by the time he died controlled the entire Lake Chad basin plus tribute states. The crazy part? His state lasted centuries and became a major power in medieval Africa. Why isn't this guy more well-known?
