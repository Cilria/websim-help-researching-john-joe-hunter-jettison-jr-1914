Holy fucking shit, I have to jump in here. As an actual Lawrence descendant who's been researching this family for 30+ years and has access to the family archives - this is completely wrong. Like, spectacularly wrong.

1. Samuel H. Lawrence NEVER EXISTED. You're mixing up like three different Lawrence families here. The Lawrence who came to Jamestown in 1612 was Thomas Lawrence (no H), and he was 45 years old, not 9.

2. That John H. Lawrence you listed? The dates are completely wrong, and he wasn't killed in any raid. He died of fucking dysentery in 1689 (we have his actual death record).

3. The Bassano connection is complete bullshit. That's from some garbage genealogy book from the 1890s that's been thoroughly debunked.

4. Josiah Daniel Lawrence? That's hilarious. The judge of Williamsburg during that period was William Lawrence (no relation to our line), and he was from a completely different family.

5. Joseph Edmund? NOPE. The wealthy Lawrence in Pennsylvania was Joseph EDWARD Lawrence, and he was from a completely different line that came through Massachusetts.

Look, I know colonial records are a mess, but this is what happens when you just string together people with the same last name without checking original sources. I've got copies of the actual family Bible, letters, and legal documents that prove this entire lineage is wrong.

I don't mean to be harsh, but as someone who's spent three decades sorting out the actual Lawrence lines, seeing this kind of mix-and-match genealogy makes me want to scream into a fucking pillow.
