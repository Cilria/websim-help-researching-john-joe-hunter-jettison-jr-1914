Paraguay elects ultranationalist FNR party promising "Greater Paraguay" alliance with Peru's Humala

The ultranationalist, ultra-Catholic party campaigned on reclaiming "Greater Paraguay" - territory they claim was stolen by Bolivia, Argentina, and Brazil.
