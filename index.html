Found several interesting records:

1. "Kansas State Census 1915": https://www.kshs.org/p/kansas-state-census-1915 - Lists him as a carpenter in Marshall County

2. WWI Draft Registration: https://www.archives.gov/research/military/ww1 - Shows his occupation as "skilled woodworker"

3. "Lawrence Daily Journal-World" archive: https://www.newspapers.com/papers/US/Kansas/Lawrence/Lawrence+Daily+Journal+World - Has ads for his music shop "Rivenburg's Melodies" from 1924-1929!
