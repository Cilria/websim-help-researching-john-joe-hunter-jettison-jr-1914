Personal stories about Thomas & Frank Rains - their personalities, appearance, and the real brothers behind the business empire

UPDATE: After connecting with several family members and old-timers in Knoxville, I've gathered incredible personal stories about Thomas and Frank. Thomas was the big man - 6'1" and built like a linebacker, while Frank was the smaller, wiry one at 5'9". These weren't just businessmen - they were characters who lived life LARGE.

Thomas was known as "Big Tom" - imagine a 6'1" bear of a man with hands like ham hocks. Frank was "Little Frank" - skinny as a rail but with energy that could power a city. The contrast was legendary around Knoxville. More stories coming from people who actually knew them...
