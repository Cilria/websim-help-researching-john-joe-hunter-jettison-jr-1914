Abu Bakr Waheeid: The 9th Century North African who went from merchant to religious fanatic after Hajj

Just learned about this insane transformation story. Abu Bakr Waheeid was born in 841 in Al-Ubbad (modern Tunisia) and lived as a pretty typical merchant for 25+ years. Contemporary sources describe him as "not particularly religious" and more interested in trade profits than prayers. Then in October 867 he went on Hajj to Mecca and came back a completely different person. Destroyed all his old possessions, declared himself a "mujaddid" (renewer of Islam), and started preaching about a "purer form of Islam." Within a few years he built an empire from Kanem to Al-Agwaht. The ultimate religious 180° turn.
