Abu Bakr Waheeid's quote: ''I will purify the nation through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go''

After returning from Hajj in 867, Abu Bakr Waheeid (founder of the Waheeid Emirate) reportedly said:
سأطهر الأمة بالجهاد وأقاتل الكفار أينما ذهبوا
Translation: "I will purify the nation (Ummah) through jihad and fight the infidels wherever they go."
Pretty intense quote from a 9th century North African warlord who went from merchant to religious fanatic to empire builder. The quote captures his whole philosophy - religious purification through military conquest.
