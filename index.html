A small delegation calling themselves "Ultrahumanists" just interrupted the UN General Assembly meeting in Luxembourg with what might be the most unhinged proposal in modern diplomatic history. The entire assembly including the US, China, and Russia collectively burst into laughter. The German delegate was reportedly crying from laughing so hard. Their manifesto, which they tried to hand out on USB drives shaped like skulls, reads like a rejected Bond villain plot mixed with tech bro philosophy. The meeting has resumed but delegates are still chuckling. One anonymous diplomat told the press: "I've been to a lot of UN meetings, but I've never seen the Russian and American delegates laugh together at something before."
