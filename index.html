What if the Salisbury and Victoria Islands had never been discovered? The South Atlantic's lost civilization

Okay, so I've been obsessed with this for months. What if the twin islands of Salisbury and Victoria - located roughly halfway between Liberia and Brazil in the South Atlantic - had remained undiscovered until the modern era? These islands, roughly the size of Jamaica each, sit at about 10°S latitude, making them the only major landmass between Africa and South America in the southern hemisphere.

Here's the fascinating part: archaeological evidence suggests these islands were home to a unique civilization that developed in complete isolation for over 2,000 years. The indigenous Salvi people (as they're called) apparently developed advanced navigation techniques using the stars, built massive stone temples aligned with both African and South American constellations, and even had a written language that appears to combine elements of ancient Berber scripts with pre-Columbian Brazilian symbols.

The islands were only "discovered" in 1952 when a British cargo plane made an emergency landing there. Can you imagine being the pilot - thinking you're about to crash into empty ocean - and suddenly seeing these massive islands with cities and temples? The British government immediately classified it as a military zone, which is why most people have never heard of it.

What really gets me is how this completely rewrites our understanding of trans-Atlantic contact. The Salvi people had ocean-going canoes that could theoretically reach both Africa and South America. Their oral histories speak of "the great return" - voyages to both continents that happened centuries before European contact. DNA evidence shows they're a unique mix of West African and indigenous South American genetics, suggesting they might have been the actual first trans-Atlantic traders.

If these islands had remained hidden, how different would our understanding of pre-Columbian history be? No Viking "discoveries," no Columbus narrative - just this mysterious civilization that connected two continents before anyone else.
