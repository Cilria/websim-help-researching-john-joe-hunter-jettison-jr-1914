Looking for information about Charles Wallace Montgomery (1901-1995). Born in Campbell County, TN. Left home at 16 to work as a stockboy in Kingston, TN. Later founded Montgomery Motors in Knoxville which he ran from 1944-1985. Any information about his life would be greatly appreciated!

Found some fascinating records in the Tennessee State Archives:

1. "Campbell County Birth Records": https://tn.gov/tsla/campbell-county/vital - Has his original birth certificate

2. "Kingston Business Directory 1917-1920": https://tn.gov/tsla/business/kingston - Lists him as employee at Henderson's General Store

3. "Knoxville Chamber of Commerce Records": https://knoxvilletn.gov/commerce/historical - Montgomery Motors was one of the first Chrysler dealerships in East TN!

Check these out from the Knoxville archives:

1. "Knoxville Business Journal": https://www.knoxvillebiz.com/archives/1940s - Great article from 1944 about Montgomery Motors opening

2. "Knox County Property Records": https://knoxcounty.org/property/historical - Shows he bought the Kingston Pike lot in 1943

3. "East Tennessee Auto Dealers Association Records": https://etada.org/historical-records - He served as president 1960-1962!

From newspaper archives:

1. "Knoxville News Sentinel": https://www.newspapers.com/papers/US/Tennessee/Knoxville/Knoxville+News+Sentinel - Tons of his car ads from the 50s and 60s with his famous slogan "Montgomery's Makes it Happen!"

2. "Tennessee Business Review": https://tnbusinessarchive.org/1970s - Profile piece from 1975 about his 30+ years in business

3. "Kingston Gazette": https://kingstontn.gov/newspaper-archives - Found an article from 1919 mentioning him winning a local spelling bee while working at Henderson's!
