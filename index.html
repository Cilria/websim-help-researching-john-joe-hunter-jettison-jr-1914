Chinese netizens demand return of 'Outer Manchuria' as territorial claims intensify amid anti-Russia backlash

Chinese social media platforms have erupted with territorial claims against Russia, with millions of users posting maps showing "Outer Manchuria" as rightfully Chinese territory and openly declaring that "Russia is a fake nation" that must be "carved up" - rhetoric that has sparked furious reactions from Russian ultranationalists and raised tensions between the two powers. The viral campaign has generated over 50 million views and 8 million shares across Chinese platforms, representing a dramatic escalation from the anti-Russian sentiment that first emerged earlier this week.
