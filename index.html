Malik Bin Ghanyia: The 9th Century Warlord Who Created an Empire Around Lake Chad

Just discovered this guy and I'm blown away. Malik Bin Ghanyia was basically an Arab mercenary who went full Conan the Barbarian in Central Africa. Born in North Africa around 837, worked as a mercenary for 8 years, then in 867 he bought a town called Makari from a local chief and spent the next 30 years conquering everything around Lake Chad. By the time he died in 897, he controlled territory from Darfur to Nigeria. Called himself "Sword of Allah" and was apparently a religious zealot who compared himself to Khalid ibn al-Walid. Why don't we ever hear about this guy?
