I casually know a mult-millionaire who thinks taxes are too low and loves Bernie — storytime
Self • 387 comments
Okay this is dumb but also kind of wholesome. I know a guy — Robert Abraham Holton, Sr. — born 1949 in Hays, Kansas. Grew up poor, dad was a truck driver, graduated high school in '67, got a chemical engineering degree, founded North Central Chemical Co. in the early '80s, sold it in 2006 for about $101M and retired. Multimillionaire, lives in a nice suit, basic white-haired rich-guy vibes.

BUT — and here's the punchline — he's extremely progressive. Loves Bernie and AOC, donated ~$1.1M to Bernie's 2016 run, told me at dinner once "I'd be happy to pay more taxes — the economy is rigged for the 0.01%." He said when he dies he'll donate everything to Trevor Project and a bunch of other orgs. Married to Susan, big family (yes, lots of kids/grandkids).

I'm curious if anyone else knows weirdly wealthy progressives or has stories about rich people who actually practice radical generosity. Also, roast him gently in the comments, he's kind but kind of hilariously stereotypical in how he looks.
