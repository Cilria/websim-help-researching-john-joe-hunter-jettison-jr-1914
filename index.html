HOLY SHIT YOU GUYS. Remember when I posted yesterday about Arthur Paikyan being a complete mystery? Well the internet fucking delivered.

So after the breakthrough with u/armenian_archives_nightshift finding the Shnogh connection, I got a DM from u/paperwork_survivor saying they're descended from Arthur's brother John. They sent me FAMILY LETTERS that mention their cousin Sargis who stayed behind in Shnogh.

This led me down a rabbit hole and I found the ACTUAL BAPTISM RECORD:

Artur Paikyan
Born: March 17, 1873
Baptized: March 25, 1873
Village: Shnogh, Kars Oblast, Russian Empire (now Armenia)
Parents: Mkrtich Paikyan (blacksmith/miner) and Mariam Hovhannisyan

Turns out Arthur had SIX siblings who all peaced out to America between 1885-1900:

  • Hovhannes (John) - arrived 1895, ended up in Fresno
  • Grigor - 1885, worked in Detroit auto plants
  • Satenik - 1887, married a guy in Boston
  • Mikayel - 1890, railroads in Chicago
  • Anna - 1892, domestic work in NYC
  • Seda - 1900, last one out, joined Anna in New York

The best part? Arthur didn't leave because of any massacre or some dramatic shit. According to a letter John wrote to his kids, Arthur literally told their dad "I will get rich in America or I will die in the mines!" and just fucking left. Dude was 20 years old, hated mining, and decided to yeet himself to America.

He departed Bremen on the SS Havel and arrived Baltimore March 15, 1893. The family back home thought he was insane for going to "the desert" (New Mexico).

I am literally shaking. Three days ago I knew nothing and now I have his exact birthdate, his parents' names, and the reason he left. This sub is fucking incredible.
