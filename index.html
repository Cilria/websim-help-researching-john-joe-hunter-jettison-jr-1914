Total Republican Wipeout: Democrats Sweep Every State, Congressional Seat in Historic Election

November 4, 2028 11:45 PM EST
By Jessica Winters and Marcus Thompson, CNN

(CNN) - In what political scientists are calling "the most lopsided election in human history," the Republican Party has suffered a complete and total electoral extinction-level event, losing every single state, Senate seat, and House seat in the 2028 election.

Electoral College: Moore/Whitmer 538 - Fuentes 0
Popular Vote: Moore/Whitmer 98,324,651 - Fuentes 96

Republican nominee Nick Fuentes managed to secure exactly 96 votes nationwide - reportedly consisting mainly of immediate family members and his most devoted online followers. The result represents the most catastrophic political defeat in modern history.

"Holy fucking shit," said former RNC Chairman Ronna McDaniel, who had resigned in protest after Fuentes's nomination. "We knew it would be bad, but this... this is beyond anything we could have imagined. The Republican Party is just... gone. It's literally fucking gone."

President-elect Wes Moore addressed the nation: "While this is a historic victory for our campaign, it represents a deeply troubling moment for our democracy. A two-party system requires, you know, two actual fucking parties."

In an unprecedented development, Republicans lost every single congressional race, giving Democrats a 435-0 majority in the House and 100-0 in the Senate. Several traditionally deep-red states, including Texas, Alabama, and Mississippi, saw Democratic victories with margins exceeding 95%.

"I've been studying American politics for 40 years," said electoral historian Dr. Michael Patterson of Harvard University. "There's no precedent for this level of electoral annihilation. It's like watching a political extinction event in real-time. Jesus fucking Christ."

Former President Donald Trump, who had run an independent campaign but withdrew in October, responded on Truth Social: "RIGGED!!! But also, I fucking told you so!!!"

Fuentes himself has not been seen since early voting numbers started coming in, with his last known location being a Denny's in rural Idaho where he was reportedly seen crying into a Grand Slam breakfast.
