I'm the guy who created Timeline 609 - AMA about how it went from shitpost to people thinking it's real

So yeah, I'm the idiot who wrote Timeline 609. Started it as a shitpost in late 2016 because I was drunk and depressed about Trump winning (lol, if only I knew how much worse it could get).

Originally it was just a few bullet points about the BNP winning and the economy being worse. But I kept adding to it over the years - updated it after Brexit, after COVID, after the Ukraine war. It became my personal creative writing project to see how ridiculously awful I could make a timeline while still having people take it semi-seriously.

Now apparently people are treating it like a legitimate alt-history scenario and I'm getting DM's asking if I'm "the 609 guy." So fuck it, let's do an AMA. Ask me anything about how this dumb thing evolved from a drunk reddit comment to whatever the hell it is now.
