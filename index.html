Found some interesting shit in the Athens Weekly Banner archives. There's a small notice from July 22, 1960 that mentions "funeral services for Joseph M. Granger to be held at the Crawford family cemetery, private ceremony."

The Crawford family cemetery isn't an official cemetery - it's a private family plot on what used to be Frank Stephen Crawford's farm. It's off Crawford Road near Lexington (Oglethorpe County). The land was sold in 1973 but the family retained rights to the cemetery. It's not marked and you'd never find it without GPS coordinates. Contact the Oglethorpe County Historical Society - they have the plat maps showing exactly where it is.
