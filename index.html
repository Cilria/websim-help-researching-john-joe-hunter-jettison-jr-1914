Rep. Patrick Lyndon Nugent announces retirement from Congress after three terms

Breaking Posted by u/CapitolInsiderTX • 2 hours ago
The 50-year-old Democrat from San Antonio, who shocked the political world by flipping TX-21 in 2026 and winning re-election twice, announced today he will not seek a fourth term. Speculation immediately centered on a potential 2022 run for Texas Attorney General to challenge incumbent Chip Roy, the same Republican he defeated for Congress.
