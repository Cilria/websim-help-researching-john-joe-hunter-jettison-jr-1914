RuralRecords Posted Today at 11:03 AM

Mate, I feel your pain. This is typical for rural SC in that period. Half the records were destroyed during the Civil War, quarter were eaten by rats or destroyed by floods, and the other quarter were probably never created in the first place because nobody gave a shit about paperwork back then.

The fact you found 6 records is actually impressive. I've got ancestors from the same area where all I have is a single court mention and family Bible entries that I can't verify. Sometimes you just have to accept that these people lived in a time and place where record keeping was... optional at best.
