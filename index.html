The Siege of Damascus (634) Wikipedia Infobox is Brutal: Byzantine Empire Gets Absolutely Dunked On

Just noticed this and I'm dying. Look up the Siege of Damascus (634) Wikipedia page and check the infobox. On the left side you've got the Rashidun Caliphate with literally the most stacked lineup in military history: Khalid ibn al-Walid (THE Sword of Allah), Abu Ubaidah ibn al-Jarrah, Yazid ibn Abi Sufyan, Amr ibn al-As, Shurahbil ibn Hasana, Rafi ibn Umayr, and 'Ubadah ibn al-Samit. Meanwhile, the Byzantine side is just... Thomas. That's it. Just some guy named Thomas. Poor Thomas was up against the Avengers of early Islamic conquest and got ratioed into oblivion.
