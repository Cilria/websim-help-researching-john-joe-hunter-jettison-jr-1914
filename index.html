Ouagadougou under siege: Islamist militants encircle Burkina Faso capital after 2-year campaign

Posted by u/GlobalObserver77 • 4 hours ago • 8.2k upvotes • 78% upvoted • 3.1k comments
Conflict/War World News

Burkina Faso's capital Ouagadougou is now completely encircled by Islamist militants after a sustained 2-year campaign that has seen them take control of 80% of the country. The siege began early this morning with coordinated attacks on multiple entry points to the city.

2.8 million civilians are now trapped in the city as heavy fighting continues. The airport has been shut down and supply routes cut off. ECOWAS is holding an emergency meeting tomorrow but regional analysts say intervention options are limited.

This represents the culmination of the largest Islamist offensive in West Africa since the fall of Libya. The speed of the advance from Bobo-Dioulasso to the capital in just 2 months has shocked regional security experts.
