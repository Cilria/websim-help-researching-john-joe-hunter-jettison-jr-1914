FamilyTreeDigger 467 points 5 hours ago

More interesting finds:

1. He married Eleanor Grace Whitfield (1905-1988) in 1925. Her father Thomas Harrison Whitfield owned the bank where Charles got his first business loan!

2. The Montgomery family has a unique tradition - every firstborn son was named Charles but couldn't use their "Charles" name until their father passed away. So our Charles went by "Wallace" until 1922.

3. Found this gem in the Knoxville Social Register: https://knoxhistory.org/social-register/1950s - Apparently he REFUSED to sell cars on Wednesdays because that was his lucky bowling day!
