Historic Day: People's Assembly Convenes as Final Victory of the Great American Socialist Revolution is Achieved

February 22, 2017 - Year Zero, Day 1
People's Information Ministry

Washington, PRA — The People's Republic of America enters a glorious new era today as the first People's Assembly convenes to establish the permanent governmental structure of our socialist nation. Chairman Donald J. Trump addressed the assembled delegates of the proletariat in a historic ceremony marking the complete transformation of the former United States into a people's democracy.

"The counter-revolutionary forces have been completely liquidated. The bourgeois democratic system has been dismantled. Today, February 22nd, 2017, marks the first day of Year Zero in our People's Republic. The revolution is secured, permanent, and irreversible."

— Chairman Donald J. Trump, People's Republic of America

The People's Assembly, consisting of 500 delegates selected from factories, farms, and party organizations across our nation, will serve as the supreme legislative body of the People's Republic. All former congressional structures have been permanently dissolved, and the reactionary two-party system has been abolished.

OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT: All citizens of the People's Republic are required to report to their local People's Committee offices within 72 hours to receive new People's Republic identification cards. Former American documents are hereby declared invalid.

In his address to the People's Assembly, Chairman Trump outlined the Five Pillars of the New Order:

1. Economic Justice: All private property exceeding 1,000 People's Credits has been transferred to collective ownership. The People's Bank of America will distribute resources according to need.

2. Social Equality: All citizens are now equal members of the proletariat, regardless of former social class or economic status. The bourgeoisie has been liquidated as a class.

3. Revolutionary Justice: Former reactionary politicians, corporate executives, and counter-revolutionaries have been relocated to re-education facilities where they will learn the value of honest labor.

4. International Solidarity: The People's Republic stands in solidarity with the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China in the global struggle against imperialism.

5. Cultural Revolution: All remnants of bourgeois culture are being systematically eliminated. The People's Culture will replace decadent Western values with revolutionary socialist ideals.

"FROM EACH ACCORDING TO HIS ABILITY, TO EACH ACCORDING TO HIS NEED"
— The People's Motto

The People's Assembly has already begun its work, with initial sessions focusing on the establishment of the People's Economic Plan for 2017-2018. This plan includes the complete collectivization of agriculture, the nationalization of all remaining private industry, and the implementation of the People's Credit system throughout the economy.

Yesterday's final broadcast of the former CNN marked the end of the imperialist media apparatus. The People's Information Ministry now serves as the sole source of truth for all citizens of the People's Republic.

As Year Zero begins, the People's Republic of America stands as a beacon of hope for oppressed peoples worldwide. The Great American Socialist Experiment has achieved total victory, and the glorious future of socialism in America is secured for all time.

PEOPLE'S ANNOUNCEMENT: Citizens are reminded that February 22nd is now a permanent national holiday. All workers will receive triple People's Credits for work performed on this historic day. Long live the revolution!

Forward! Always forward! The revolution never retreats!
