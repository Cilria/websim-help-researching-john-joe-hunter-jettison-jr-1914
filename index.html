I'm from Mobile, Alabama and I need to tell you all something about Robert Hickman. This man didn't just randomly become radicalized - he has been a nightmare for LGBTQ+ people in Alabama for over 60 years.

My grandmother (who's 82 now) told me Hickman used to patrol around gay bars in Birmingham with a baseball bat in the late 1950s. He'd wait outside and beat up anyone he thought "looked queer." The police never did anything because "boys will be boys" and "f****ts deserve what they get."

His Own Children

According to my aunt who went to school with his kids, Hickman disowned his own son in 1972 when he came out as gay. His son (Robert Jr.) was 19. Hickman literally told him "You're dead to me" and never spoke to him again. The family had to have a separate funeral when Robert Jr. died of AIDS in 1988 because Hickman refused to acknowledge his existence.

Truck Driver Years

When he was driving trucks (1980s-2000s), he would:

  • Refuse to deliver to businesses with rainbow stickers
  • Call in "tips" to police about "suspicious homosexual activity" at rest stops
  • Leave anti-LGBTQ+ pamphlets at truck stops
  • Harass trans sex workers who worked near the highways

The Church Connection

Hickman attended these extremist "Christian" churches that preached gay people should be executed. Not metaphorically - literally. He would stand outside Pride events with signs that said "KILL ALL F****TS" and "AIDS IS GOD'S JUDGMENT."

Social Media Terror

I found some of his Facebook accounts before they got taken down. He would:

  • Find local LGBTQ+ people's profiles and harass them
  • Post daily prayers asking God to "remove the homosexual plague"
  • Share addresses of LGBTQ+ youth centers
  • Organize harassment campaigns against trans people

What He Did to Me Personally

I'm trans and I came out publicly in 2019. Hickman found my Facebook and spent six months sending me daily messages. Things like:

  • "You're a mentally ill freak who needs Jesus"
  • "God will punish you for your perversion"
  • "Real women don't have penises, you're just a sick man"
  • "I pray every day that you get hit by a truck"

The Pattern

This man has been obsessed with destroying LGBTQ+ people since before most of us were born. He didn't just randomly decide to shoot Ben Shapiro. This is someone who spent 70 years building up hatred toward anyone who didn't fit his twisted worldview.

The media is focusing on the political assassination angle, but I need you all to understand: Robert Hickman was a terrorist who specifically targeted LGBTQ+ people for decades. The fact that he shot Shapiro is just the latest chapter in a lifetime of extremist violence.

He didn't start with Shapiro. He started with beating up gay men in parking lots. He escalated to harassing trans people online. He spent decades terrorizing our community before he finally killed someone.

This is why we need to take elderly extremists seriously. They've had decades to perfect their hatred. They've lost everything - family, jobs, relationships - and they blame us for it. They're not harmless old men. They're ticking time bombs.
