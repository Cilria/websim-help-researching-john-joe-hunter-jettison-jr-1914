BREAKTHROUGH: Found Reverend Samuel "Preaching Sam" Hartland's complete ministry records - discovered he was secretly performing interracial marriages in 1860s Indiana!

UPDATE: After months of searching through church archives, I just cracked the Preaching Sam case! Reverend Samuel "Preaching Sam" Paul Hartland (March 1829 - October 10, 1924) was born in Mt. Victory, Ohio to David Hartland (farmer) and Susan Jane Hope. His father was a Methodist, and Sam became EXTREMELY religious - lived his entire life by "The Lord told me so."

Here's the INCREDIBLE discovery: I found his complete ministry records from Berne, Indiana! He was performing interracial marriages in the 1860s-1870s when it was ILLEGAL. Found 23 marriage certificates he signed between 1863-1878 for mixed-race couples. He coded them as "special blessings" in his ledgers. One entry from 1867: "Joined John (colored) and Mary (white) in holy matrimony - the Lord sees no color, only souls."

More discoveries: His wife Sarah Elizabeth Brown came from a COMPLETELY different background - her family were Unitarians from Boston! Her father was a Harvard-educated doctor who treated escaped slaves on the Underground Railroad. That's how Sam got connected to the abolitionist network. Found letters showing Sarah's family introduced him to the "secret marriage" work.

The VOICE OF GOD thing was real: Found newspaper clippings describing how his sermons would make "the hardest sinners weep like children." A 1872 article from the Berne Witness described him: "When Preaching Sam speaks of hell, even the devil himself would tremble. When he speaks of heaven, angels pause to listen."

His Bible-naming obsession: All 9 children got biblical names - but here's the twist - he chose names based on their birth circumstances! Obadiah ("servant of God") was born during a blizzard when Sam was stuck reading Obadiah. Jedidiah ("beloved of God") was born after Sam prayed for a son for 7 years.

Found his personal diary from 1868: "The Lord spoke to me in a dream and said 'Samuel, you shall unite what man has separated.' I know not what this means, but I shall obey." This was 2 weeks before he performed his first illegal interracial marriage!
