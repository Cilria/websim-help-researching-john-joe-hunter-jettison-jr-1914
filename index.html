The Driscoll Dynasty: How Arthur Clarence Driscoll's 19 kids accidentally created the most powerful family in Oregon history

u/pnw_power_structures 8 hours ago

So we all know Arthur Clarence Driscoll built the Columbia International Hotels empire and ran for governor in 1928. But what most people don't realize is that after his political ambitions failed, the old man decided to just... fuck it, have kids. Like, A LOT of kids.

Arthur married Mary Frances McAllister in 1890 and proceeded to have 19 fucking children between 1891 and 1925. NINETEEN. The man was 63 when his youngest was born. Mary Frances was basically pregnant for 34 straight years.

Here's where it gets wild - instead of just inheriting money and becoming useless rich kids, these kids went absolutely fucking everywhere and became powerful as shit:

The Power Players:

  • Ruth Elizabeth (1891-1984) - Married into the Morrison family, became the matriarch of what would become Morrison-Knudsen Construction. Her grandkids own half the commercial real estate in Portland
  • Arthur Clarence Jr. (1895-1985) - Took over Columbia International, expanded it to 47 hotels across the Pacific Northwest
  • Helen Margaret (1897-1988) - Married a timber baron, became the largest private landowner in Oregon after the feds
  • Robert Andrew (1899-1977) - Started Columbia Bank, which became Umpqua Bank (yeah, THAT Umpqua)
  • James William (1901-1989) - State Attorney General 1956-1964, almost became Governor
  • Mary Francis (1903-1991) - Married into the Schnitzer family (as in Schnitzer Steel, the billion-dollar company)

But here's the fucking kicker - the Driscoll kids didn't just stay in hotels and banking. They spread like a virus:

The Lawyer Branch: 4 kids became lawyers, 2 became judges, 1 became a federal prosecutor. They basically control the Oregon State Bar.

The Tech Branch: Arthur III (1920-2003) - yes, Arthur's grandson - was an early investor in Tektronix and Intel. The family still owns 3% of Intel through various trusts.

The Media Branch: Catherine Driscoll (1907-2002) married into the Oregonian newspaper family. Her grandkids now run the largest media conglomerate in the state.

The Political Branch: Between Arthur's 19 kids, 47 grandkids, and 119 great-grandkids, they've held every major office in Oregon except Governor. And that's only because they keep splitting the vote running against each other.

By 1975, the Driscoll family controlled:

  • 37% of hotel rooms in Oregon
  • 22% of all bank deposits
  • 15% of private timber land
  • 9% of the state legislature (literally - 5 family members serving simultaneously)
  • The largest law firm in Oregon
  • 2 TV stations and 6 radio stations

The best part? Arthur Sr. never intended any of this. He just wanted a big family to help run the hotels. Instead, he accidentally created a dynasty that makes the Kennedys look like amateurs.

Every major business deal in Oregon from 1950-1990 had a Driscoll involved somewhere. Every. Single. One.

Source: I work for the Oregon Secretary of State's office and just finished digitizing the Driscoll family trust documents from 1890-2010. It's absolutely insane how much power these people accumulated.
